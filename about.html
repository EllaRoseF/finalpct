About Pacific Trails Resort

Welcome to Pacific Trails Resort, a family-owned and operated establishment that has been providing unforgettable experiences to guests since 1920. Nestled along the stunning coastline of northern California, our resort combines the natural beauty of our surroundings with world-class amenities and impeccable service to create a truly unique and memorable stay.
At our resort, we take pride in being a family-run business and we strive to make each guest feel like a member of our family. From the moment you arrive, our dedicated staff will go above and beyond to ensure that your stay is nothing short of extraordinary.
So why wait? Book your stay at our luxurious resort on the coast of northern California today and experience the ultimate in relaxation, adventure, and family hospitality.
